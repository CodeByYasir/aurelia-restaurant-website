The Ember Room

16 – 28 Guests

Named for the custom open-fire grill at its centre, the Ember Room offers a theatrical dining experience built around live-fire cookery. Exposed brick, aged oak tables, and copper fixtures create a dramatic yet intimate atmosphere.

The Aurelia Suite

28 – 40 Guests

Our largest private space, The Aurelia Suite spans the full east wing of the restaurant. With its own entrance, bar, and lounge area, it offers complete seclusion for weddings, milestone events, and large corporate dinners. Fully modular — configure for banquet, boardroom, or reception.
